If you're too busy to fly to DC, you can still play an important role in politics. OneCall is making it easier and more effective to get in touch with your representatives, who are weighing in on the issues you care deeply about. These cover a wide range of topics like climate change, race relations, LGBTQ rights, social safety nets, immigration, gun control, campaign finance reform, and so many others.

OneCall has taken all the pain out of contacting your representative, by automatically connecting you to their office during your morning commute! Every day, you'll receive one call (at the time you've specified) that tells you about an important issue that needs your support. If you agree with the idea, then we'll automatically connect you to your representative so you can briefly express your views. It's as easy as picking up the phone.
